**TL;DR**

We prioritize your privacy and collect minimal data. We do not log IP addresses, store cookies, or maintain logs. The only information we keep is your roadmap and checklist progress (saved locally on your device). Everything else is intentionally NOT collected to safeguard your privacy.
{{< /callout >}}

### Information We Do Not Collect
Our site is designed with your privacy as a top priority:
- **No IP Addresses:** We do not log any IP addresses.
- **No Usage Statistics:** We do not collect any analytics data such as page views, device type, location, or referrer information.
- **No Cookies:** We do not store cookies on your device.
- **No Third-Party Services:** We do not load any external services or third-party libraries that could compromise your personal information.

### Local Storage of Your Progress
To provide a useful experience, we allow you to save your roadmap and checklist progress and dark mode preferences. This data is stored **locally on your device** using your browser's built-in localStorage feature:
- It remains entirely on your device.
- It is **never sent to our servers** or shared with anyone.
- You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's storage or cache for this site.

### Security
Since no data is transmitted to or stored on our servers, there is no risk of data breaches on our end related to your usage.

### Open Source Transparency
Our entire website is open source and available for review on [GitHub](https://github.com/BeginnerPrivacy) or [Codeberg](https://codeberg.org/BeginnerPrivacy). We encourage you to inspect it yourself or have a trusted technical advisor review it.

### Changes to This Policy
We will update this policy if our practices change. The last updated date will be noted at the bottom.

### Have Questions?
If you have any questions about this policy or our practices, please [contact us](../contact).
