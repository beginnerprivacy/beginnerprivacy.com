### The Future of Beginner Privacy
Beginner Privacy began as a for-profit website, hence the .com domain. We’ve now transitioned into a non-profit-focused project. Our main goal is simply to help people improve their privacy and security.

<br>

Going forward, Beginner Privacy will be maintained as a side project, which it has been for some time already. This will be our last transparency report for a while. That said, we will continue to publish articles, add new tools, welcome contributors, and improve the site.
